How do I create a tournament?

It's simple! Just fill out the form and you'll be on your way to starting your own League of Legends showdown. Fill out what you want your tournament to be called, along with the Summoner Name of your organizer (probably you). Next, go back to the tournaments page and find your new entry to start filling in your participants, teams, and all other information. Then when you're ready, just go ahead and click play to start the bracket of your very own League of Legends tournament.
